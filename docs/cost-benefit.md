## 5. ROI Calculation

### Formula
ROI = (Total Benefits - Total Costs) / Total Costs × 100

### Calculation
- Total Development Cost: $18,130
- Annual Operational Cost: $1,620
- **Total Cost (Year 1): $19,750**

- Annual Tangible Benefits: $11,706
- **Total Benefits (3 Years): $35,118**

- Total Cost (3 Years): $19,750 + ($1,620 × 2) = $22,990

ROI = (35,118 - 22,990) / 22,990 × 100
ROI = 12,128 / 22,990 × 100
ROI = 52.75%

### **3-Year ROI: 52.75%** ✅

---

## 6. Recommendation

### Decision: ✅ PROCEED

**Justification:**
The Hospital/Clinic Management System shows a positive ROI of 52.75% over 3 years. The system pays for itself within approximately 2 years through time savings and error reduction alone. The intangible benefits such as improved patient satisfaction and professional image add further long-term value that is not captured in the financial calculations.

The use of free hosting (GitHub Pages) and free CI/CD (GitHub Actions) keeps operational costs extremely low at only $135/month, making this a highly cost-effective solution for the clinic.

## 7. Case Study

"If this system is used by 500 patients per month, the impact is significant. Each patient saves approximately 25 minutes in appointment booking and record retrieval time. This translates to 208 hours saved per month across all patients. At a conservative value of $10 per hour of time saved, the system generates $2,080 in patient time savings every month, or $24,960 per year. Combined with staff efficiency gains of $11,706 per year, the total annual impact reaches $36,666 — nearly double the total 3-year operational cost of the system."
