# Privacy Note — Hospital / Clinic Management System

## Data Collected

| Data Type | Purpose | Collected From |
|-----------|---------|---------------|
| Patient name | Identification | Registration form |
| Patient age | Medical records | Registration form |
| Contact number | Appointment reminders | Registration form |
| Email address | Notifications | Registration form |
| Medical history | Treatment records | Doctor input |
| Appointment details | Scheduling | Booking form |
| Billing information | Payment records | Receptionist input |

## Why We Collect This Data
- To provide accurate medical care to patients
- To schedule and manage appointments efficiently
- To maintain medical history for continuity of care
- To process billing and payments

## Data Retention Policy
- Patient records are retained for 10 years as required by Philippine health laws
- Appointment records are retained for 5 years
- Billing records are retained for 7 years
- Deleted accounts are purged after 30 days

## User Rights
- **Right to Access** — Patients can request a copy of their data
- **Right to Correction** — Patients can request corrections to their data
- **Right to Deletion** — Patients can request deletion of their data
- **Right to Portability** — Patients can request their data in a readable format

## Data Security
- All data is encrypted at rest and in transit
- Access is restricted using role-based access control
- No patient data is shared with third parties
- All access to patient records is logged
